# Umi Landing Design System

## Direction

Warm operational product site for restaurants. The design should feel clear, capable, human, and lightly playful. Avoid a severe consulting deck tone.

## References To Mix

Use the discipline of a SaaS product page plus the warmth of a modern restaurant brand. Do not copy a single reference. Prefer confident whitespace, soft rounded surfaces, real food/restaurant imagery, and a few operational UI details.

## Color Tokens

- Deep blue: `#223979`
- Ink: `#142142`
- Warm paper: `#FBF7EF`
- Strong surface: `#FFFDF8`
- Accent: `#E7A85B`
- Soft blue: `#7692CB`

## Typography

- Primary text: Nunito Sans, bold and friendly.
- Display accent: Fraunces, used sparingly for emphasis.
- Avoid serious editorial serif paragraphs.
- Letter spacing should stay at `0`.

## Layout Principles

- One clear hero with a shader atmosphere and a real-image placeholder.
- Sections after the hero should be warm, mostly opaque, and easy to scan.
- Prefer fewer cards with stronger hierarchy over dense bordered grids.
- Every major product area should reserve space for future real imagery.

## Motion And Effects

- Keep the WebGL shader as a technical hero effect, not a full-page distraction.
- Use CSS and simple React motion for reveals and hover states.
- Avoid scroll hijacking, constant movement, and decorative effects that compete with copy.

## Do

- Use rounded photo placeholders until real restaurant assets are available.
- Make product value visible in plain language.
- Keep buttons, chips, cards, and forms tactile but restrained.

## Do Not

- Do not return to the all-dark cockpit look across the whole page.
- Do not add more libraries for layout effects that CSS can handle.
- Do not use generic purple gradients, bento clutter, or serif-heavy consulting typography.
