# Promotion Criteria

Only promote a new root-level skill when the pattern is clearly reusable across more than one Umi project or cross-product task.

## Gate
- The pattern recurs across more than one successful trace in `routing-ledger.md`.
- The procedure is stable and narrower than an architecture role.
- The owning repo or root slice is stable.
- The artifact would not duplicate a project-local skill.
- The new skill would reduce repeated cross-workspace setup or placement mistakes.

## Do not promote when
- the task is first-contact or specific to one product repo
- the real need is a durable fact in `CLAUDE.md`
- the task depends on ongoing architecture judgment
- the pattern belongs inside a project-local `.claude/`, not root
